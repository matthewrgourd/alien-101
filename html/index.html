About me

I'm a hedonistic person curious about new technology and passionate about electronic music. I love expressing my feelings for music through visual artistry. My MacBook with VDMX software can plug into any projector or I can bring my own to gigs if needed.

Portfolio

I regularly attend and perform at AV events around London. I have provided live visuals for many electronica performances, including featured shows at StowLab and Crux AV.
