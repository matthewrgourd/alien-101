My work as a visual artist

I'm a former teacher based in London and when I perform I'm Alien 101. I'm a member of VJ London and CRUX AV whose regular meet-ups I attend in London. I perform using a MacBook and VDMX software and own my own projector which I can bring to gigs if needed. This year I've performed at Crux - AV Link Jam part of Splice Festival @ Stour Space and Stow-Lab @ Trap.
