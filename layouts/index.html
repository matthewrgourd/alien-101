About

Alien 101 is a hedonist curious about new technology and passionate about electronic music. She loves to express her feelings for music through visual artistry. Her MacBook with VDMX software can plug into any projector as well as her own which can be brought to gigs if needed.

Portfolio

Alien 101 regularly attends and performs at AV events around London. She has provided live visuals for many electronica performances, including featured shows at StowLab and Crux AV.
